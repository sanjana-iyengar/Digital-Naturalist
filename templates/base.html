Who is DIGITAL NATURALIST


A naturalist is someone who studies the patterns of nature, identifies a different kind of flora and fauna in nature. Being able to identify the flora and fauna around us often leads to an interest in protecting wild spaces, and collecting and sharing information about the species we see. Digital Naturalism unites biologists, designers, engineers, and artists to build and analyze new devices. It focuses on interacting with species in new ways.
